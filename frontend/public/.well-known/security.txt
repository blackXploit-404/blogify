Blogify Security / Vulnerability Disclosure Program

Hello Security Researchers,

Thank you for taking the time to test and report vulnerabilities for Blogify.

If you discover any security vulnerabilities or bugs, please report them responsibly by sending an email to:

security[at]surajitsen.live


 In-Scope Assets

blogify.surajitsen.live

---

Vulnerability Categories

Since our platform is currently in development, we are only accepting valid vulnerabilities with Medium to High severity at this time. Examples include:

* Authentication / OTP bypass
* Malicious file upload
* Non-blind SSRF
* Reflected or DOM-based XSS
* Account takeover
* Any other vulnerabilities that could significantly impact security

Additional targets and vulnerability categories may be added in the future.

---

### Hall of Fame Criteria

Researchers who submit valid reports may be listed in our Hall of Fame.
Please include the following details in your report:

* Your preferred name or handle
* Email address
* LinkedIn profile (optional)

---

Rules

* Please perform testing responsibly.
* Do **not** perform any kind of **DDoS or service disruption attacks**.
* Do not attempt to access or modify other users' data without authorization.
